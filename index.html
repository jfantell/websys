About

John is an Information Technology & Web Science and Business Management student at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. John is a highly motivated, proactive individual who has taken on various leadership roles in his school, his job, and his community. He is just as passionate about designing technologies as he is about applying existing technologies to craft smarter, innovative solutions. As evidence, he is currently working with a team of four other individuals to design an application that will aid users with little to no web knowledge in finding and ranking the best deals on items they wish to purchase.

John has a strong aptitude for problem-solving, and over the summer he had the opportunity to work at Viacom as an IT service desk analyst. He provided technical assistance to several hundred company employees and contractors on a weekly basis with issues relating to VDI/VPN, Microsoft Office, LDAP, Active Directory, hardware, and network performance. Currently, John is working with two Professors from the Lally School of Management on a research project which aims to study and categorize the relevant literature examining the interface between new product development and suppliers. This will be the second major research project he has taken part in since high school, the first being a 3-year study investigating how to minimize glare caused by photovoltaic cells.

As part of a commitment to better serve the Rensselaer student body, John serves on the Executive Board – a student-run body that is responsible for overseeing the Student Union’s $4.9 million budget for club and event programming. He also serves on the Rensselaer Union Rules and Elections, and Policies Committees.
